History

Electric Picnic is an annual arts and music festival which has been staged since 2004 at Stradbelly Hall in Stradbelly, County Laois, Ireland.
Electric Picnic differs from other festivals in Ireland in that the music choice is more electronic than the other mainstream events, includes international acts such as The Chemical Brothers, Nick Cave, Sonic Youth and more!
In the beginning, Electic Picnic started of as a one day event in 2004 before growing to a weekend long fesitval within a year.
The festival also incorporates different attractions that include a 24 hour cinema tent, silent disco etc.
